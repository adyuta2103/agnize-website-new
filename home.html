Vimseltinib (Romvimza) Approved for Tenosynovial Giant Cell Tumor

In early 2025, the FDA approved Vimseltinib (brand name Romvimza), a targeted CSF1R kinase inhibitor for adults with tenosynovial giant cell tumor (TGCT) who are not eligible for surgery. Unlike traditional therapies, Vimseltinib interrupts key signalling pathways in tumor-associated macrophages.
